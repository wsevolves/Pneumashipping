Cargo Solutions
Sea Freight PNEUMA offers a single-source solution to the complexities of ocean freight, giving you the freedom to use your energy where you need it most. PNEUMA with it’s well renowned business partners are uniquely positioned to synchronize the supply chain needs end to end at all levels. Our flexible range of ocean services meets the needs of any customer.
LCL Consolidation PNEUMA consolidates the cargo and import/ export through our networks, have convenient regional container freight stations (CFS) located globally, providing service availability for less than container loads (LCL) and consolidation services, either by sailing, weekly, every other week or in accordance with your requirements. We facilitate communications and monitoring of your shipments with your vendors, shippers, trading networks and buying offices and perform consolidation services according to your specific instructions. We provide booking status, loading reports and arrival notification for all shipments to ensure that LCL cargo location is known every step of the way.
Air Freight If you are weighed by time constraints then there is no option other than sending your items by air freight. When you are in hurry to send air freight entrust your job to PNEUMA Shipping LLC. With years of experience and well connected networks we will be able to meet your deadlines leaving you with the freedom to dedicate your valuable time for other important matters. We offer tailor made services to suit the needs of each customer with the best prized solutions. Founding principle of PNEUMA Shipping LLC is to provide world–class services with a personal attention. We offer fast, safe and reliable service to almost all destinations globally
Road Freight We can deliver your cargo anywhere in and around UAE and other GCC countries through road. We provide a safe and cost effective inland transport which ensure flexibility to cope up with the customer demand. Road freight offers several advantages, including flexibility in terms of pickup and delivery locations, faster transit times compared to other modes of transport, and the ability to transport a wide range of goods. By providing accurate and detailed information about your shipment, you can ensure that you receive the most accurate and competitive quote for road freight
Cargo Insurance Don't leave your cargo unprotected. Cargo Insurance comes in different types with different exclusions and you need the proper insurance for your specific shipments. Universal Cargo's friendly and professional staff will take the complications out of insuring your cargo so you can be assured that your imports and exports are covered in the event of loss or damage
